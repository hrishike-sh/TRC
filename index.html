WHAT IS TRC?
Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal, founded in 1934, is a Public Charitable Trust rooted in the patriotic spirit of India's independence movement. Over the past 80 years, it has grown significantly, now serving over 35,000 students and expanding from Mumbai to major metro cities.
